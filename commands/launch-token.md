# /launch-token

Spans **BUILD → SECURE (gate) → OPERATE (gate) → GROW**. Read-only / key-safe; OPERATE informational (counsel/CPA).

## Steps (sequenced, gated)
1. **BUILD** — mint/program (Token-2022 extensions as needed), distribution + claim flow. ([`build.md`](../skill/clusters/build.md))
2. **SECURE (gate)** — audit mint/claim, custody of mint + upgrade authority, drainer-safe claim UX. ([`secure.md`](../skill/clusters/secure.md))
3. **OPERATE (gate)** — securities/classification posture + tax treatment by jurisdiction (e.g. US income recognition; India 30% VDA + 1% TDS) + treasury accounting; delegate to `crypto-legal` + `solana-accounting`. **Flag counsel/CPA.** ([`operate.md`](../skill/clusters/operate.md))
4. **GROW** — TGE plan, airdrop/eligibility, distribution/listings, community, post-launch comms. ([`grow.md`](../skill/clusters/grow.md))

## Output
- A gated launch plan; per-gate status; jurisdiction-specific legal/tax notes (counsel-flagged); TGE/GTM plan.
- Gate to launch: audit clean; legal/tax posture documented with counsel; claim UX drainer-reviewed. "Current as of 2026-06."
